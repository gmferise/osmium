Osmium Privacy Policy

It is understandable to be concerned when an application is asking access to manage your files.
We highly value your security and privacy, so this document will explain to you what exactly we need access to and why.

Data Collection and Storage

Our app does not store or collect any data from your Google Account, the files on your Google Account, or your browser.
It serves only as a user-friendly interface to input and view data stored in Google Sheets.

Google Permissions and Scopes

When performing actions with Google's API, we must enable certain scopes.
A scope is essentially a package of permissions.
We use as few as possible, and we don't use all of the permissions each scope grants.
Here are the scopes we use and the individual permissions we use from them.

Scope Full Permissions Used Permissions Explanation
/auth/spreadsheets See, edit, create, and delete your spreadsheets in Google Drive See, edit, and create only Used to create new databases and edit the contents of existing databases
/auth/drive.file View and manage Google Drive files and folders that you have opened or created with this app View and manage Used to view databases created with our app
/auth/drive See, edit, create, and delete all of your Google Drive files See and edit only Used to search for and edit databases that were not created with our app or aren't owned by you
